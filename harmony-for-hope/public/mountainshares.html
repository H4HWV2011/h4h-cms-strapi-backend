The Challenge We're Solving

82% of local spending leaves Appalachian communities

Money flows out to distant corporations, weakening our local economy and reducing opportunities for our neighbors.

MountainShares keeps wealth circulating locally

Every MountainShare spent stays in our community, strengthening local businesses and creating lasting prosperity.

How It Works

🤝

EARN

Contribute to your community through volunteer work, local services, or supporting neighbors. Every hour of community service earns you MountainShares that recognize your contribution.

🏪

SPEND

Support neighbors at local shops, restaurants, and service providers. Use your MountainShares like cash at participating businesses throughout Appalachia.

🗳️

GOVERN

Shape the future together through community decision-making. MountainShare holders have a voice in how the system grows and serves our region.
